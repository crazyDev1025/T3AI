Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about T3 Identity.

  • Decentralized Identity is a framework to digital identity management that utilizes cryptography, blockchain and other decentralized technologies in a trusted way that prioritizes user privacy and security. It uses unique digital identifiers associated with a user's identity and verifiable credentials that are self-owned, independent and enable trusted data exchange.
  • Digital identifiers are rooted in a trust system (foundation establishing trust between decentralized systems). They are decentrally stored on blockchain or peer-to-peer networks without data centers or an intermediary. User data and information are divided into fragments and distributed throughout different nodes on a network.
  • This gives individuals ownership and control over their identities and how their personal data is used
